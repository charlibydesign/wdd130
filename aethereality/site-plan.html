Overview

Purpose

This website's purpose is to allow my customers an opportunity to gather information on what products the shop sells currently, what products are in stock, when products will be restocked, and what products are upcoming. The site will also serve as a way for customers to decide what they want to purchase prior to going to the actual shop to make the purchase.

Audience

The audience for this website are men and women ranging in age from 14 - 99. These people are fun loving, hard working, and love the great outdoors. They spend a lot of their time camping, fishing, hiking, skiing, sledding, and doing other fun activities outdoors. As many of their activities can, and will, be done during the colder months of the year, they are the perfect audience for the handmade hats and beanies this shop sells.
